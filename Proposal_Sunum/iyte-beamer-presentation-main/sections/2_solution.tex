\section{Proposed Solution: VoxMed}

\begin{frame}{Introducing VoxMed}
    \textbf{VoxMed} is an AI-driven, voice-activated mobile assistant that acts as a real-time health and safety safeguard.
    \vspace{0.3cm}
    \begin{itemize}
        \item \textbf{Instant Packaging OCR:} Captures food ingredients or medication labels and extracts all text automatically.
        \item \textbf{Smart Health Alignment:} Compares extracted ingredients with the user's allergy and medication profile.
        \item \textbf{Voice-First Interface:} Completely hands-free control via Speech-to-Text (STT) and Text-to-Speech (TTS).
        \item \textbf{Intelligent AI Reasoning:} Analyzes text using local Large Language Models (LLM) to determine safety.
    \end{itemize}
\end{frame}

\begin{frame}{Interactive Features}
    \begin{itemize}
        \item \textbf{Allergen Alert:} Checks for allergens (peanuts, milk, gluten, etc.) in food products.
        \item \textbf{Medication Advisor:} Reads out dosages, warnings, and warns about interactions.
        \item \textbf{Multilingual Support:} Automatically translates and reasons across different languages (e.g., scanning foreign packaging and warning in Turkish/English).
        \item \textbf{Follow-up Conversations:} Users can voice-ask questions: \textit{"How much sugar is inside?"} or \textit{"Does this cause drowsiness?"} and receive instant spoken answers.
    \end{itemize}
\end{frame}
